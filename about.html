Kalpataru Institute Of Tecnology

Kalpataru Institute of Technology was established in 1986 with a view to provide technical education to the rural youth. The college is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belgavi and is recognized by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. The college has an excellent team of qualified, experienced and dedicated faculty members. The 28 years old Institute is equiped with all the required infrastructure.The college campus is located on the outskirts of the town at a distance of about 2 Km from Railway station and Bus Stand.

KIT came into existence with the establishment of the institution in the year 1986. The college is presently headed by Dr.Guru Murthy Who is duly supported by well qualified and experienced staff members. The staff members of the department are actively involved in teaching of basic applications of science and Electronics Engineering. Our department organized National Seminar on Global Environmental Issues and Solutions on 14th August 2015 associating with Physics and Mathematics departments. Department faculty have published 13 papers in international journals, one paper in Indian Journal and 12 papers in National/International Conferences/National Symposium/Workshop. Our department has aim to bring research centre.
